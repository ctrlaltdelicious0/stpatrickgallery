Dear STEM Batch 2025,

Congratulations, my dear students. Maligayang araw ng pagtatapos sa inyo.

Watching you grow from curious, sometimes uncertain learners into confident, capable young scientists has been one of the greatest honors of my teaching journey. Hindi madali ang STEM track—napakaraming pagsubok, puyat, at pressure. But you made it. And seeing you now, abot-kamay niyo na ang mga pangarap na dati ay drawing lang sa class notebook ninyo.

Napagtagumpayan ninyo ito, and I know you’ll continue to conquer life’s bigger challenges. Ipagpatuloy ninyo ang pag-aaral, kahit minsan o madalas mahirap, and let kindness and humility guide your every step. That will always be your greatest strength.

It was a privilege to walk beside you through this journey, even for a short while, mula sa unang quiz hanggang sa pinakahuling laughtrip sa class. I hope you carry not just the lessons, but the memories, friendships, and courage you discovered along the way.

You will always have a place in my heart, Batch 2025. Keep that STEM spark alive wherever life takes you, and go where your passion leads, pero huwag kalimutang isama ang puso sa bawat desisyong gagawin. The world needs more kind and curious minds like yours.

With all my heart,

- Sir Karch
